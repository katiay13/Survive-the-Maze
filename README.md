Survive the Maze  
A unity game that revolves around a user navigating through a maze and obstacles inside it to reach the exit

About:  
Survive the Maze is a game that challenges the player to find their way through a maze. The player must avoid obstacles and enemies while also collecting coins
to set new high scores. The player can pick up tokens which can help them throughout the game however they must also watch out for tokens that can be harmful. 

Features:  
-A maze filled with obstacles and an enemy that chases the player  
-Coins that the player can pick up on their way out of the maze  
-Tokens which can either help the player or hinder them  

Controls:  
-Use WASD keys to move the player in different directions  
-Hold right click to change camera angle
